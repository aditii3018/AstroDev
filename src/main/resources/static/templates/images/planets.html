Overview of the Planets

Our solar system consists of eight major planets, each with unique characteristics:

Mercury: The closest planet to the Sun.

Venus: The hottest planet in our solar system.

Earth: The only planet known to support life.

Mars: The red planet, known for its surface features.

Jupiter: The largest planet in our solar system.

Saturn: Known for its beautiful rings.

Uranus: The tilted planet with a unique rotation.

Neptune: The farthest planet from the Sun.
